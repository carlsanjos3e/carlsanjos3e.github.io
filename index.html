Liham para sa aking sinisinta,

Happy

Birthday

Baba!

Today marks another trip for you around the sun. The past ten months have been some of the best months of my life. We experienced our first year of college, celebrated our first Christmas and New Year together, and made so many memories that I couldn't fit them all into your birthday collage; and now, here we are celebrating your 20th birthday together.

If there is one thing that I am certain of in this world, it would be you. You were always there for me through everything. Like on the day of my first interview for the Student Council screening, you supported me and gave me the strength to face it head on; and during the times where I thought I could no longer go on, and was filled with doubts and uncertainties, you were always there with the right things say to quell those doubts and to help me press on.

Of all the things I've ever wished for you, the one thing that I've included in all of those wishes, was your happiness. I understand that your childhood has not treated you as well as you would've wanted it to. But I hope I can give you the happiness that you truly deserve, because someone like you deserves the world.

I'll be here to spend your next solar revolution with you, and each one after that, until the earth stops spinning and the sun can shine no more.

Sa muli, maligayang kaarawan, sinta.

"Mahal na mahal kita."
